Open Code

Learn Web Development

    Web Development is one of the most in demand occupations today with pay ranges for entry level web developers in New York City ranging from $41,847 - $83,373 and an average salary of $60,074 according to Payscale.Com. The US Bureau of Labor Statistics notes that web developer employment is projected to grow a full 20 percent from 2012 to 2022, faster than the average for all other occupations

    LaGuardia Community College is introducing our new Web Development program, open code, to help our students get the skills to take advantage of the great opportunities in this field. Open code. continues the college’s tradition of delivering innovative, career focused training programs that lead to student success. With instructors who are practitioners in the tech industry, along with the supports that you can only get at a mission driven institution like LaGuardia, we can help you open the doors to an exciting career in the high demand field of web development.

    By the end of the open code program, you will have learned how to code in multiple program languages, met industry professionals inside and outside of your classroom, created your own websites and apps and gained an understanding of what it’s really going to take for you to move into one of the most in-demand careers of the 21st century. If you’re ready to make the leap, we’ll be here to make sure that you have the right tools, guidance and support to break into the tech industry and to see yourself moving forward in a career that has a world of possibilities.

    Are you ready?
